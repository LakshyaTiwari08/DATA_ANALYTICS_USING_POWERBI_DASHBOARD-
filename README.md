# DATA_ANALYTICS_USING_POWERBI_DASHBOARD-
An interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing product sales, regional performance, customer behavior, KPIs, and business insights using Power Query, DAX, and data visualization techniques.

  Product Sales & Regional Performance Dashboard | Power BI

  About the Project

This project showcases an **interactive Business Intelligence dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze product sales, regional performance, customer purchasing behavior, and key business metrics.

The primary objective of this dashboard is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations, helping businesses make informed, data-driven decisions. It allows users to monitor sales performance, identify top-performing products and regions, evaluate customer segments, and track important KPIs in a single, user-friendly interface.

---

 Project Objectives

The dashboard was built to answer key business questions such as:

* Which regions generate the highest sales?
* Which products contribute the most revenue?
* How do customer segments impact overall sales?
* What is the effect of discounts and shipping costs on business performance?
* Which payment methods are most preferred by customers?
* How can business performance be monitored using KPIs?

---

Dashboard Overview

This Power BI report consists of multiple interactive pages designed for detailed business analysis.

Executive Sales Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of business performance with important KPIs including:

* Total Sales
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Sales
* Total Discounts
* Total Shipping Cost
* Total Returns

It also includes:

* Regional Sales Analysis
* Revenue by Payment Method
* Product Category Distribution
* Interactive Filters (Region, Product, Month, Customer Type)

---

Product & Customer Analysis

This page focuses on understanding customer behavior and product performance.

Features include:

* Region-wise Product Sales Table
* Sales vs Quantity Scatter Plot
* Customer Sales Driver Analysis
* Regional Discount Analysis

---

Customer Revenue Analysis

Provides insights into:

* Revenue generated by different customer segments
* Overall business observations
* Key business insights derived from the data

---

Performance Monitoring Dashboard

Designed for management reporting using KPI visuals such as:

* Sales Performance Gauge
* Sales KPI Monitoring
* Target vs Actual Performance

---

Region Drill-through Report

Allows users to drill into individual regions and analyze:

* Product-wise Sales
* Quantity Sold
* Total Revenue
